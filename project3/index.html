In Cedarville's halls, a leader stands tall,
With wisdom's grace and martial arts' might,
The president, revered by one and all,
In karate's realm, he shines ever bright.

A black-belt master, swift in every move,
With discipline forged through years of toil,
His prowess echoes, as the crowds approve,
In every strike, a tale of strength and foil.

Yet 'tis not just in combat's fierce domain,
His leadership, a beacon strong and true,
Guiding minds through wisdom's noble reign,
In Cedarville's embrace, his vision grew.

So let us hail this president of might,
Whose karate skills and wisdom shine bright.
